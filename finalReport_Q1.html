Section 03 · New Policies Effective Q2
Effective Q2 FY26
New Financial Policies

The following policies are effective beginning Q2 (April 2026). Submissions that do not comply will be denied. Please read carefully and share with your full shura.

New · Q2

Separate receipts for YM purchases

All YM-related expenses must be purchased on their own, separate receipt. Do not mix personal and YM purchases on the same receipt — submissions with personal items on the same receipt will not be approved.

New · Q2

Venmo or Zelle reimbursements only

We are streamlining reimbursements to Venmo (@Young-Muslims) or Zelle only. The check option is being removed. After submitting your JotForm, please immediately send your Venmo or Zelle request so we can process it quickly.

New · Q2

Amazon & online purchases — PDF receipts

Screenshots of Amazon orders or any online purchase receipts are no longer accepted. You must download the receipt as a PDF from the retailer and upload that file to the form.

  • Amazon → Order Details → Print/Download as PDF
  • Any online vendor → PDF invoice or receipt
  • Exception: Zelle or Venmo transfer screenshots are still fine
New · Q2

Itemized details — items only, no prices

In the Itemized Details field, list only the items purchased. Do not include individual item prices and do not include tax — just a clean list of what was bought.

  • ✓ Balloons, tablecloths, string lights, streamers
  • ✗ Balloons $10, tablecloths $15 + tax

These updates are designed to make Finance processing faster for everyone. The Venmo/Zelle change in particular means we can approve and pay out the same Tuesday your submission comes in — no delays waiting for checks. Thank you for your patience as we tighten these processes up.

Section 04 · Policy Reminders
Ongoing Policies
Friendly Reminders

These are not new — but we've seen consistent issues this quarter. Please review.

Reminder

What counts as an Internal Transfer?

We've seen many submissions incorrectly use Internal Transfer as the transaction type. Internal Transfers are only for moving money between your team's existing YM accounts.

✓ Valid internal transfers:

YM Bank
↔
Cash Box
·
YM Bank
↔
Personal Bank Acct
·
Cash Box
↔
Personal Bank Acct

✗ This is NOT an internal transfer:

Personal funds
→
Personal Bank Acct (YM portion)

⬆ This is Income to Personal Bank Account. Please resubmit correctly.

Reminder

Tax is included in your total

We cover tax. Do not exclude tax from your expense total. Your total amount submitted should include tax — enter the full amount from your receipt.
We are a nonprofit organization and are eligible for tax expemptions, if you need it for large purchases please email finance team.

Required

No receipt = no approval

Every expense submission requires a receipt or bank statement. We will not approve any submission without one. For paper receipts, make sure the full receipt is clearly visible — if we can't read it, it will be denied.

Reminder

Expense Details format

Follow this format in the Expense Details field:

MM/DD/YY · Event Name · Expense Category

Example: 1/9/26 NJ Gala – Decorations

Required

Use your YM email

All JotForm submissions must use your YM email (e.g. name@ymsisters.com). Personal email submissions will not be accepted. Dashboard access requests from personal emails will also be denied. Email admin@ymsisters.com if you need a YM email.

Action Required

Missing starting balances — contact your SR Finance Lead NOW

It is April 2026. Starting balances were due in January–February. Many NNs still have not submitted theirs, which means their dashboards cannot accurately reflect their Q1 finances. Please contact your SR Finance Lead or SRC immediately. We will be reaching out individually to all outstanding chapters.
