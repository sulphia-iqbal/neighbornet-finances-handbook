NN Spotlight
YM 571 · Crescent Café

Each quarter we spotlight an NN whose fundraising creativity and execution can inspire chapters across the country إن شاء الله. This quarter's NN spotlight goes to YM 571 from the NJ Central SubRegion for their Ramadan Crescent Café initiative — a community café built from the ground up inside their masjid!

Total Income
$3,428
Total Expenses
$824.84
Net Profit
$2,603.16
How It Worked

YM 571 partnered with their masjid, MCGP, to run a community café during Taraweeh prayers. They operated after 8 rakats on weekends and every night during the last 10 nights of Ramadan.

The menu included hot & iced coffee, lattes, matcha, hot chocolate, and refreshers. Payments were accepted via Zelle, Venmo, cash, and Square tap-to-pay, helping keep the payment process smooth and accessible for everyone.

The masjid provided most equipment. The NN covered ingredients, cups, and supplies — keeping overhead lean. A portion of income came from community donations in addition to drink sales.

Merch Initiative

YM 571 also ran a merch campaign — hoodies and stickers — open throughout April!

Hoodies (via Tapstitch) Cost $23.80 · Sold $35
Stickers (via Canva) Cost $1 · Sold $2

Merch revenue still being collected — final totals TBD.

✦
Key Takeaway for Other NNs

YM 571's café was successful because they recognized a real need in their community and turned it into a thoughtful fundraiser. During Taraweeh, many people appreciate having coffee or other drinks to stay energized — and YM 571 found a trendy, practical way to serve that need. By offering something people already wanted, they were able to raise meaningful funds for their NN while also creating a service that benefited the community. Pay attention to what your community naturally needs or enjoys — then build a simple, low-overhead fundraiser around it and show them what YM is all about إن شاء الله.

Section 05 · New Financial Policies
New Financial Policies
New Financial Policies

The following policies are effective beginning Q2 (May 2026). Submissions that do not comply will be denied. Please read carefully and share with your full shura.

New · Q2

Separate receipts for YM purchases

All YM-related expenses must be purchased on their own, separate receipt. Do not mix personal and YM purchases on the same receipt — submissions with personal items on the same receipt will not be approved.

New · Q2

Amazon & online purchases — PDF receipts

Screenshots of Amazon orders or any online purchase receipts are no longer accepted. You must download the receipt as a PDF from the retailer and upload that file to the form.

  • Amazon → Order Details → Print/Download as PDF
  • Any online vendor → PDF invoice or receipt
New · Q2

Itemized details — items only, no prices

In the Itemized Details field, list only the items purchased. Do not include individual item prices and do not include tax — just a clean list of what was bought.

  • ✓ Balloons, tablecloths, string lights, streamers
  • ✗ Balloons $10, tablecloths $15 + tax
✦

Making sure your JotForm submissions follow these policies helps us help you get your reimbursement processed more quickly 😎. Thank you for your patience!

Section 06 · Friendly Reminders
Friendly Reminders
Friendly Reminders

A few important reminders as we head into Q2 — these policies help keep everything running smoothly for everyone. We appreciate you taking a moment to review them! 🤍

Action Required

Missing starting balances — contact your SR Finance Lead NOW

It is April 2026. Starting balances were due in January–February. Many NNs still have not submitted theirs, which means their dashboards cannot accurately reflect their Q1 finances. Please contact your SR Finance Lead or SRC immediately. We will be reaching out individually to all outstanding chapters.

Required

No receipt = no approval

Every expense submission requires a receipt or bank statement. We will not approve any submission without one. For paper receipts, make sure the full receipt is clearly visible — if we can't read it, it will be denied.

Required

Use your YM email

All JotForm submissions must use your YM email (e.g. name@ymsisters.com). Personal email submissions will not be accepted. Dashboard access requests from personal emails will also be denied. Email admin@ymsisters.com if you need a YM email.

Reminder

What counts as an Internal Transfer?

We've seen many submissions incorrectly use Internal Transfer as the transaction type. Internal Transfers are only for moving money between your team's existing YM accounts.

✓ Valid internal transfers:

YM Bank
↔
Cash Box
·
YM Bank
↔
Personal Bank Acct
·
Cash Box
↔
Personal Bank Acct

✗ This is NOT an internal transfer:

Personal funds
→
Personal Bank Acct (YM portion)

⬆ This is Income to Personal Bank Account. Please resubmit correctly.

Reminder

Tax is included in your total

We cover tax. Do not exclude tax from your expense total. Your total amount submitted should include tax — enter the full amount from your receipt.
We are a nonprofit organization and are eligible for tax exemptions, if you need it for large purchases please email finance team.

Reminder

Expense Details format

Follow this format in the Expense Details field:

MM/DD/YY · Event Name · Expense Category

Example: 1/9/26 NJ Gala – Decorations
